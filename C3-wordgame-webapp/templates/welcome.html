How to play...

You will be provided with a word of seven letters or more which we'll call the "sourceword". You are then to think up seven three-or-more letter words made up from the letters contained within the sourceword, ensuring that:

  • Each word is only made up from the letters contained within the sourceword.
  • Each word exists within the dictionary (i.e., it's a “real” word).
  • The words all have three letters or more.
  • There are no duplicate words.
  • None of the seven words is the source word.
  • And don't forget: case is NOT significiant (Pet, PET, and pet are the *same* word).
